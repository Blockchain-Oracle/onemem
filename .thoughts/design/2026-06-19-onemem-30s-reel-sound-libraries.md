# OneMem — 30s Reel · Sound Libraries to Browse

**Purpose:** A curated list of places to find the underscore + sound design for the 30-second X launch reel. Browse, pick what fits the brief, send back the link / file. Then we license + ship.

**What we want** (full criteria in §8 of the designer brief): 90–95 BPM, A-minor-to-C-major lift, arp-driven motion-graphics underscore, no drums in the first 8s, no vocals, no risers. Jon Hopkins / Nils Frahm / Tycho / Apple-reveal / Linear-launch lineage.

## Sound design / one-shot accents (separate from underscore)

The brief calls for **one quiet verification chime around 24.5s** and optionally **one soft click when the product name first appears at 13s**. These are sound design, not music — pull from:

- **Freesound.org** — search: `notification chime soft single`, `ui click subtle`, `key detent`
- **99sounds.org** — free packs, look for "UI Designer Sounds" or "Interface"
- **Splice Sounds** — search: "ambient SFX UI chime", "futuristic interface single"

Whatever you pick, **the verification chime must be quieter than any other accent.** Memory is the hero; proof is the confidence layer.

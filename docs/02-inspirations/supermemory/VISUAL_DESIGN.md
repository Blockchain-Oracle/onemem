# Supermemory — Visual Design

Captured 2026-05-23. Source-of-truth screenshots in `./screenshots/`.

## Primary screenshots

- `screenshots/01-landing-full.png` — https://supermemory.ai full landing

## Brand colors

- Body bg: `#F5F9FF` (very light blue tint — distinctive)
- Body text: `#0B1015` (near-black)
- Heading color: `#0B1015`
- Primary blue accent: `#0763EE` / `#0452DB` / `#2563EB` (multiple blue steps)
- Light blue fill: `#F4F8FF`, `#F0F5FD`
- Dark surface: `#0B1015`, `#0A0E13`
- Brown/amber accent: `#92400E` (unusual — feels like a warning color used decoratively)

## Typography

- H1: `Space Grotesk`, 58px, weight 500 — text: "Superintelligence requires Supermemory"
- H2: `Space Grotesk`, 40px
- Body: `DM Sans`, 14px
- Two-font system: display grotesk + utility sans

## Component patterns observed

- Light-blue-tinted background instead of pure white (subtle but distinctive)
- Space Grotesk gives a slightly geometric/technical headline feel
- DM Sans for body keeps it neutral
- Blue-on-blue palette (multiple blue tints)
- Square buttons (`border-radius: 0`)

## Layout signature

- Light hero with tinted bg, headline + sub + CTA
- Cards layered with subtle elevation
- Dark sections for product detail (inverted near-black panels)

## UX moments worth borrowing

1. **Tinted bg `#F5F9FF`** instead of pure white — instant differentiation from the white-SaaS sea.
2. **Space Grotesk + DM Sans pairing** — solid free-font combo (both Google Fonts).
3. **Multi-step blue palette** — three blue tints used together gives depth without introducing a second hue.

## What this teaches OneMem

- Free font pairing `Space Grotesk + DM Sans` is a credible alternative to Ratch/TWK Everett/GT America.
- Tinted backgrounds (cream OR light-blue) are the way out of the white-SaaS look.
- Multiple tints of one accent color > multiple accent colors.

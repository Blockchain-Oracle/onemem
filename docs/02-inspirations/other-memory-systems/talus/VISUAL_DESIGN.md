# Talus — Visual Design

Captured 2026-05-23. Source-of-truth screenshots in `./screenshots/`.

## Primary screenshots

- `screenshots/01-landing-full.png` — https://talus.network landing

## Brand colors

- Body bg: `#ECEBE9` (warm light gray — paper / linen feel)
- Body text/headline: `#000000`
- Dark surface: `#000000`
- Transparent overlays: `rgba(0,0,0,0.2)`
- Button color: white (on dark surface)

## Typography

- Body family: `Borna` (custom by Suitcase Type Foundry — geometric sans)
- H1: `BelfastGrotesk`, 70px, weight 400 — text: "Build AI Agents that Earn While You Sleep"
- Two-font system: BelfastGrotesk display + Borna body

## Component patterns observed

- Warm light-gray bg (similar tonal family as MemWal cream, Langfuse sand)
- Square / minimal buttons (`border-radius: 0`)
- All-black text on warm-gray = high contrast, no chromatic accents

## Layout signature

- Hero with two-line H1, single CTA
- Minimal chrome, lots of whitespace
- Brand mark + nav at top, very restrained

## What this teaches OneMem

- Another point in the warm-neutral-bg space (after MemWal cream, Walrus inverse, Langfuse sand, Letta cool gray). The whole verifiable-AI / agent-economy space converges on warm neutrals.
- BelfastGrotesk + Borna is a paid pair — free analogs: General Sans (display) + Geist (body).
- Confirms the pattern: serious Web3 + AI brands lean monochrome + one warm-neutral bg, no chromatic accents.

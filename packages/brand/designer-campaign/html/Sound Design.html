039.4–14.8s

Decentralized by default.

On screen: OneMem → Seal → Walrus/MemWal → Sui stack assembles

  • A soft filtered-noise encrypted sweep rises as each plate snaps into the stack.
  • Three short stabs punctuate encrypted · portable · owned.

0414.8–22.2s

One namespace. Every runtime.

On screen: four proof cards — Claude writes, Codex recalls, agents coordinate, traces persist

  • Busiest section: a tight memory-write click on every card entry, four-on-the-floor.
  • Distinct click timbres for write (lime), search (sui), coordinate (violet), trace (verify green).
  • A subtle hat/tick layer keeps forward momentum without rising in volume.
